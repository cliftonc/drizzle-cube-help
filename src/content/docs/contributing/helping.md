---
title: Contributing Guide
---

It would be fantastic if you are willing to help with the project.  Most importantly we want people who are keen to make a positive contribution to the community and help to make this project better.  I will not tolerate abusive or non-inclusive behaviour, as I tell my children, if you don't have something positive to contribute please do it somewhere else.

The contribution guidelines are in the main drizzle-cube repo:  [Contribution Guidelines](https://github.com/cliftonc/drizzle-cube/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)
